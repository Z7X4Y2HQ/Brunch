Two pictures will be given.
First picture is Dot Pattern picture. In this picture all letters and their respective dot pattern will be given.
Second picture is Color Pattern picture. In this picture, there will be pattern similar with the dot picture but colors will be used instead and no letters will be provided.
So with the help of dot pattern picture you have to guess the character of color pattern.
Once you guess all the color patterns letters, a sentence will be formed.
Write the sentence to solve the puzzle.
